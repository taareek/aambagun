Dataset

We have adopted the publicly available PlantVillage dataset to train and evaluate our proposed model for classifying tomato leaf diseases. PlantVillage is a public repository of various leaf images, including those of tomato leaves, which aims to enhance plant health through the development of mobile diagnostics. This dataset contains images of 10 types of tomato leaves (including healthy and diseased) and is partially unbalanced. Therefore, we selected approximately 1,000 images from each category, creating a customized dataset with a total of around 10,000 images. The categories consist of Bacterial Spot, Early Blight, Healthy, Leaf Mold, Septoria Leaf Spot, Late Blight, Mosaic Virus, Target Spot, Spider Mites, and Yellow Leaf Curl Virus. All images are in RGB format with dimensions of 256x256 pixels.
